# Authoritative sources for laws-refresh

Reference list of sources, organised by topic. Use these in priority order when refreshing laws files.

---

## Capital gains, slabs, rebates, surcharge

### Primary
- **Income Tax Department:** https://incometaxindia.gov.in
- **Union Budget portal:** https://indiabudget.gov.in (Finance Bill, FAQs, Memorandum Explaining the Provisions, Speech)
- **CBDT notifications:** searchable on incometaxindia.gov.in (look for the latest CII notification, e.g. "Notification No. 70/2025")
- **Press Information Bureau (PIB):** https://www.pib.gov.in (search "Budget 2025" or "Budget 2026" for official press releases)

### Secondary (cross-check only)
- ClearTax (https://cleartax.in) — particularly clear breakdowns of Budget changes
- Bajaj Finserv tax content (https://www.bajajfinserv.in/investments/income-tax-slabs)
- LegalSuvidha, Tax2Win, Finnovate — tax-focused publications

---

## Mutual fund rules and categorisation

### Primary
- **SEBI:** https://www.sebi.gov.in — search "mutual fund master circular" for the consolidated rules
- **AMFI:** https://www.amfiindia.com — categorisation rules, top-100 list (large/mid/small cap), daily NAV data
- **AMFI scheme details:** scheme-by-scheme tax treatment classification

### Secondary
- Fund factsheet PDFs (monthly) from individual AMC websites
- Mirae Asset / HDFC AMC / etc. annual tax reckoners (issued Apr/May each year)
- Value Research, Moneycontrol — for fund-level data, not for rule sources

---

## Specific schemes

### PPF
- **Primary:** Ministry of Finance quarterly rate notifications (search "PPF interest rate Q-X FY-YYYY-YY notification")
- **Primary:** Public Provident Fund Scheme, 2019 rules (notification at https://www.indiapost.gov.in)
- **Secondary:** ClearTax PPF page, PaisaBazaar PPF page

### EPF / VPF
- **Primary:** EPFO press releases at https://www.epfindia.gov.in
- **Primary:** Annual interest rate announcement (typically Feb-Mar each year, sometimes split announcement)
- **Secondary:** ClearTax EPF page, news coverage of EPFO Central Board of Trustees meetings

### NPS
- **Primary:** PFRDA (https://www.pfrda.org.in) — circulars, notifications
- **Primary:** NPS Trust (https://www.npstrust.org.in/benefits-of-nps) — current tax benefits page
- **Primary:** NSDL eGov NPS portal (https://www.npscra.nsdl.co.in) — for Tier 1/Tier 2 rules
- **Secondary:** ClearTax 80CCD page, finlecture.in, taxbuddy.com

### SCSS
- **Primary:** Ministry of Finance quarterly notifications (same channel as PPF)
- **Primary:** SCSS Rules notification on indiapost.gov.in
- **Secondary:** ClearTax SCSS page, Bajaj Finserv SCSS page

### Sovereign Gold Bonds
- **Primary:** RBI press releases at https://www.rbi.org.in (search "Sovereign Gold Bond")
- **Primary:** RBI premature redemption calendar (issued half-yearly)
- **Primary:** PIB releases on SGB tranche announcements (when issuances happen)
- **Note:** SGB is currently paused; check periodically for resumption announcements

## Insurance

### Primary
- **IRDAI:** https://www.irdai.gov.in — circulars on IRDA-regulated products, ULIP rules
- **Insurance Act 1938** — for Section 10(10D) base
- **Income Tax Act sections** for premium deduction (80C, 80D, 80DD, 80U)

### Secondary
- Insurance company circulars (HDFC Life, ICICI Pru, etc. — for their own product details)

## Income Tax Act 2025 transition

Beginning 1 April 2026, the Income Tax Act 2025 replaces the IT Act 1961. Section numbers will renumber. For at least 2 years post-transition:
- Cross-reference both old and new section numbers
- Watch for clarifications from CBDT on transitional issues
- Flag any section-number change as MEDIUM confidence

Useful tracking: incometaxindia.gov.in will publish a section-mapping table during the transition. Check periodically.

## Search query patterns that work

For the most recent Budget changes:
- "Budget 2026 capital gains changes"
- "FY 2026-27 income tax slabs new regime"
- "Union Budget 2026 PPF EPF NPS"

For quarterly rate revisions:
- "PPF interest rate Q[1/2/3/4] FY 2025-26"
- "Small savings interest rate January 2026"
- "SCSS interest rate FY 2025-26"

For specific scheme updates:
- "Sovereign gold bond 2026 RBI announcement"
- "EPFO interest rate FY 2025-26 announcement"
- "NPS 80CCD(2) employer contribution FY 2026-27"

For SEBI/AMFI rule changes:
- "SEBI mutual fund category amendment 2026"
- "AMFI top 100 list December 2025"

## Avoid

- **Forum / community sites** without primary citation (Reddit, Quora, etc.) — fine for context, never as primary source
- **AI-summary sites** without dated, sourced content
- **Outdated tax-saving guides** — many websites have stale content from earlier FYs that ranks well in search
- **Marketing pages** disguised as tax info (insurance company "tax savings" pages often conflate selling with informing)
- **Single-source MEDIUM claims:** if only one secondary publication mentions a change and no primary source confirms, do not propose the change

---

## When to mark a proposed change as flagged for user judgment

- **Multiple sources interpret a rule differently** (e.g. some sources say SGB secondary market buyers retain maturity exemption, others say not)
- **A rule recently changed and is being applied retrospectively** — ambiguity about pre/post pivot date
- **Section number renumbering** during IT Act 2025 transition
- **TDS rate changes** that may apply prospectively only — verify effective date
- **Threshold changes** where the effective FY is unclear
